# Fishing God: Aquaculture App Redesign PRD ## 1. Vision & Goals Redesign the "Fishing God" app to be a premium yet practical tool for Indian fish and shrimp farmers. The UI must be highly readable in outdoor conditions, easy to use with one hand, and performant on low-end devices. ## 2. Visual System (The "Aquaculture" System) - **Primary Color:** Deep Pond Green (#004D40) - representing depth and health. - **Secondary/Accent:** Algae Teal (#26A69A) - for secondary actions and highlights. - **Surface:** Warm Sand / Off-White (#FDFBF7) - to reduce glare and feel grounded. - **Success:** Vibrant Emerald (#4CAF50) - for healthy parameters. - **Warning:** Sun-Drenched Amber (#FFB300) - for alerts. - **Error:** Deep Clay Red (#D32F2F) - for critical issues. - **Typography:** Strong sans-serif (Inter or similar) with clear hierarchy and large body text for readability. - **Radius:** 12px for cards and buttons to balance modern feel with approachability. ## 3. Design Principles - **Field-Friendly:** High contrast, large tap targets (min 48px), and scannable layouts. - **Trustworthy & Grounded:** Avoid overly "techy" gradients; use solid colors and meaningful iconography. - **Consistent Hierarchy:** Standardized headers, cards, and data visualization patterns. ## 4. Key Screens to Redesign 1. **Auth Screen:** Branded welcome with phone-based login/signup. 2. **Home Dashboard:** Weather advisory, harvest countdown, and quick action grid. 3. **Species List:** Searchable cards with key environmental stats. 4. **Species Detail:** Deep dive into biological and economic parameters. 5. **Economics Input:** Optimized form for ROI calculations. 6. **Economics Result:** High-impact dashboard with benefit-cost ratios. 7. **Geo Suitability Map:** Interactive map with suitability scoring. 8. **Water Quality:** Data entry and trend tracking. 9. **Market Prices:** Real-time pricing with trend indicators. 10. **Equipment & Feed Catalogs:** Clean product listings with supplier CTAs. 11. **Profile & Ponds Management:** Complete farmer and farm management suite. ## 5. Technical Considerations - **Dark Mode Support:** All colors must have defined dark variants. - **Low Connectivity:** Design for clear loading and offline states. - **Localization:** Layouts must accommodate Hindi and other regional languages (flexible container widths).
